Our Story

More than food —
it's an experience.

We are a locally owned and operated small business, rooted in El Salvadoran culture and located in the heart of Old Colorado City. Everything on our menu is made fresh, in-house — from our signature pupusas to our homemade beverages.

Using organic, non-GMO ingredients and offering a 100% gluten-free menu — recognized by the National Celiac Association — we take pride in every plate we serve.

House Specialty

Pupusas

Hand-pressed, made-to-order corn masa cakes — each approximately 6 inches, made with 100% organic corn masa. Choose from 15+ fillings: pork (revueltas), beef, chicken, spicy carnitas, loroco, zucchini & chile poblano, roasted Pueblo chile, black bean, mushroom, jalapeño, and multiple vegan options. Served with curtido and homemade salsa.

Starting at $6.50 each

Traditional

Tostadas & Entrees

Two Tostadas Salvadorenos topped with meat and vegetables, served with 2 sides ($13.50). Also try Chilaquillas Salvadorenos — four oven-baked stuffed tortillas with 2 sides ($15.00).

Beverages

Drinks & Cocktails

Horchata, Agua Fresca, and handcrafted cocktails — mojitos, margaritas, Bloody Marys, Cuba Libres, and more. All cocktails $9 or under. Beer and wine available.

Weekend Brunch

Weekend Brunch

Saturday & Sunday 11am–2pm. The Traditional Plate — eggs, beans, plantains & chorizo ($11.50). Also: Salvadoran Omelet, Pupusa Ranchera, Breakfast Street Tacos, and more.
