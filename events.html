What to Expect

The Monse's Experience

Warm, Vibrant Setting

Dine inside our cozy restaurant or enjoy our dog-friendly outdoor patio. Our colorful, culturally-rich space creates the perfect backdrop for any celebration.

Authentic Salvadoran Menu

Treat your guests to handmade pupusas, tamales, tostadas, and more — all prepared fresh with organic, non-GMO ingredients. 100% gluten-free kitchen, vegan options available.

Handcrafted Cocktails

Complement your event with our full bar — mojitos, margaritas, Bloody Marys, Cuba Libres, beer, wine, and more. All cocktails $9 or under.

Groups of All Sizes

Our staff is experienced in accommodating large groups. For groups of 6 or more, our team will help coordinate seating and service to make your event run smoothly.

Yelp Top 100 Quality

Impress your guests with food that earned national recognition. One of only three Colorado restaurants named to Yelp's Top 100 Places to Eat in the U.S. in 2019.

Old Colorado City Location

Conveniently located at 115 S. 25th Street with free parking in the adjacent lot near W. Cucharras Street. Easy to find, easy to park, easy to love.
